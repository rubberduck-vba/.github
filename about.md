# About

![logo](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c0f97c4c-ca88-4508-bd55-73e946ea1d20)

Rubberduck VBA Software Solutions Inc. is a Canadian software company (in the process of incorporation), focused on the development of technical tools for professionals.

Our approach is grounded in a set of simple principles:
technical rigor, transparency, respect for users and contributors, and a commitment to building solutions designed to endure over time.

## Our philosophy

We believe software tools should:

- be designed with care and responsibility;
- respect privacy by default;
- rely on open standards whenever possible;
- recognize and respect the work of communities and contributors.

These principles guide:

- our technical decisions;
- our publication practices;
- our relationships with users, partners, and third‑party developers.

## Community and contributions

Part of our work takes place within collaborative and open‑source ecosystems.  
We place particular importance on:

- clarity of licensing;
- proper recognition of contributions;
- healthy coexistence between community initiatives and commercial activities.

Applicable terms are published transparently, as appropriate.

## Evolution and transparency

Certain information relating to our products, services, or infrastructure is intentionally disclosed on a progressive basis.

This approach is intended to:
- avoid premature confusion;
- preserve the quality and consistency of public communications;
- ensure that stated commitments are accurate and sustainable.

> ⚠️ Public pages are expected to evolve as our activities develop.

### The future: an Open‑Core model

The next evolution of Rubberduck adopts an Open‑Core model, with its spirit, core, and engine remaining firmly anchored in the GPLv3 license, ensuring openness, fairness, and long‑term sustainability.

> 👉 Make sure you are subscribed to [rubberduckvba.blog](rubberduckvba.blog) to be notified when everything is explained in an upcoming blog post.
