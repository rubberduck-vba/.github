# Privacy Policy  
**Temporary Draft — Preliminary Version**

Last updated: to be specified  
Solutions Logicielles Rubberduck VBA Inc.

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Privacy Policy describes the general approach adopted by
Solutions Logicielles Rubberduck VBA Inc. (the “Company”) with respect to privacy
and personal information.

It applies to software, services, websites, code repositories, and
communications operated by the Company, in their current or future form.

This version is intentionally **high-level and transitional**. It will be
expanded and clarified as public products and services are formally announced.

## 2. Core Principles

The Company is guided by the following principles:

- **Privacy by default**  
  No non-essential data collection is performed without functional or legal
  necessity.

- **Progressive transparency**  
  Data practices will be documented publicly when they become relevant to
  users.

- **Consent and control**  
  Where personal information is involved, the Company aims for clear,
  understandable consent and allows withdrawal where applicable.

- **Data minimization**  
  Data is limited to what is reasonably necessary for operation, security, or
  support.

- **Respect for contributors**  
  Contributions (code, documentation, ideas) remain subject to the licenses and
  rights chosen by their authors.

## 3. Personal Information

At this stage, the Company does **not define specific categories of personal
information collected**, as no public product or service has yet been formally
announced.

When personal information is processed, the Company commits to:

- clearly describing the categories involved;
- explaining the purposes of processing;
- complying with applicable privacy and data protection laws.

## 4. Users and Contributors

Users and contributors may reasonably expect that:

- their contributions will not be exploited in a misleading or abusive manner;
- their work remains governed by applicable licenses;
- no hidden monitoring or intrusive data collection will be introduced without
  clear communication.

Detailed terms will be published prior to the deployment of any relevant
features.

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure

The Company does not sell personal information.

Any potential disclosure to third parties (e.g. hosting or technical services)
will be limited to what is strictly necessary and contractually governed where
applicable.

## 6. Policy Evolution

This policy is expected to evolve.

Any more detailed or binding version will be published publicly and dated. The
most recent version shall prevail.

## 7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or matters concerning user and contributor rights:

- **privacy@rubberduckvba.com**  
- **legal@rubberduckvba.com**

The Company aims to maintain consistent French and English versions.
